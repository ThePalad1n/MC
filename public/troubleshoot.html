MAC: Unidentified Developer



This is a very common issue and this is a walk through on how to resolve it. First you will want to go to system preferences. Next click on Security & Privacy. Then click on the General tab and click Open anyway. Now the forge installer should open.

Now click the instructions page up at the top to continue.
